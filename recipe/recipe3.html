Tuna Melt Sliders

Ingredients

  • 15 Hawaiian-style slider buns, split in half horizontally, such as King's HawaiianÂ®
  • 3 (5 ounce) cans chunk light tuna in water, drained
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped celery
  • 1/3 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons dill pickle relish
  • 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 12 sharp Cheddar cheese slices
  • 5 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon chopped dried dill
  • 1 clove garlic, grated

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C). Lightly spray a 9x13-inch baking dish with cooking spray. Place bottom halves of rolls in the prepared baking dish, cut side up.
  2. Stir together tuna, celery, mayonnaise, relish, salt, and pepper in a bowl until evenly combined.
  3. Top rolls with 6 slices of Cheddar cheese, spread tuna mixture on top of the Cheddar, and cover with remaining 6 slices Cheddar.
  4. Place top halves of rolls on top, cut-side down. Stir together butter, Dijon, dill, and garlic until combined. Brush all but 1 1/2 tablespoons butter mixture evenly over the tops of rolls. Loosely cover rolls with aluminum foil.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. Remove and discard foil. Continue baking at 350 degrees F (180 degrees C) until cheese is completely melted, about 5 minutes more. Brush remaining butter mixture evenly over rolls. Serve immediately.
